Game of Games begins-your life

Experience the thrill of Call of Duty on the go

The game stays fresh with monthly "Seasons." We are currently in Season 2: Lunar Charge, which brought: New Maps: Updates to classics like Shipment and the introduction of Building 21 for tactical play. Mythic Gear: Ultra-rare "Mythic" weapon blueprints (like the Lachmann-556) that feature custom animations and death effects. Crossover Events: Frequent collaborations with brands like Gundam or Street Fighter allow for unique operator skins.

LUNAR DRAGON

The Lunar Dragon Battle Pass Vault allows players to unlock legendary items originally featured in the 2024 Lunar New Year event. It’s perfect if you missed out on the "Year of the Dragon" aesthetics previously.

GRAVEYARD SHIFT

Originally launched as a massive Halloween-themed update, the Graveyard Shift event focuses on the return of Zombies and a dark, eerie aesthetic for the game’s most iconic maps.

SIREN SON

The core of the "Siren's Song" event is the Mythic Drop, which features the first-ever female Mythic Operator. Unlike standard skins, this one evolves as you play.

GIRLS FRONTLINE

The Girls' Frontline (GFL) collaboration is legendary in CODM because it bridges the gap between tactical military shooters and "waifu" gacha aesthetics.

DIGITAL DUSK

Unlike the traditional military feel of the game, Digital Dusk leans heavily into cyberpunk and retro-futurism. Everything from the operator skins to the weapon kill effects features neon purples, deep blues, and digital distortion patterns.

SOLDIER'S TALE

A Soldier’s Tale" moves away from modern military aesthetics and dives into a twisted, storybook world. The characters and weapons are all reimagined versions of classic fairy tales like Alice in Wonderland and Cinderella.
